// DESIGN BREAKDOWN

Built with intent.

Every panel of this jersey was designed to say something about who wears it.

01 — FRONT

The crest, front and centre.

The Faculty crest sits on the left chest above the MEDICINE wordmark — bold, condensed, unmistakable from across the field. A mandarin collar with a clean front placket gives it the edge of a competition kit, not a plain tee.

  • Faculty of Medicine crest, left chest
  • Condensed "MEDICINE" wordmark
  • Notch collar, contrast piping
  • Ribbed cuffs, white trim
02 — BACK

Wear the faculty on your back.

"Faculty of Medicine, University of Ruhuna" sits above a bold "Sports Club" script — the full identity, printed where a teammate reads it first: your back, on the run.

  • "FACULTY OF MEDICINE" + university line
  • Signature "Sports Club" script mark
  • Room reserved below for name & number
03 — PATTERN

A pulse, not a print.

Diagonal fractures run across a deep teal base — half lightning bolt, half heartbeat trace. The gradient runs darkest at the hem and lifts toward the shoulder, so the jersey reads calm at rest and sharp in motion.

  • Deep teal-navy base colourway
  • Directional diagonal fracture pattern
  • Tonal gradient, dark hem to lit shoulder
